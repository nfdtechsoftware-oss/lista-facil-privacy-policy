Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 08, 2025
In summary: Easy List is an application that fully respects your privacy. We don't collect, track, or share any data. Everything stays on your device, under your control.

1. Introduction

NFDTech ("we", "our" or "company") developed the Easy List app ("app" or "application") as a free application. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information if anyone decides to use our service.

2. Information Collection and Use

Easy List does NOT collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

The application works 100% offline and stores all your data locally on your device. Specifically:

  • Shopping Lists: All created lists are stored exclusively in your device's local storage
  • Items: All items added to your lists are stored locally
  • Preferences: Your settings and preferences are stored locally

3. Locally Stored Data

The application uses the device's local storage (SharedPreferences on Android) to save:

  • Shopping list names
  • List items
  • Item status (purchased/not purchased)
  • List categories and emojis
  • Creation and update dates

Important: This data remains exclusively on your device and is not sent to any external server.

4. App Permissions

Easy List does NOT request any special device permissions. The app does not access:

  • Your location
  • Your contacts
  • Your camera
  • Your microphone
  • Your photos
  • Your network/internet connection (the app works completely offline)

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with third parties because we don't collect any data. All information remains on your device.

6. Security

We value your trust in providing us with your information. Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security depends on the device's own security. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device protected with a password/PIN
  • Keeping your operating system updated
  • Regularly backing up your device data

7. Children's Data

The application does not intentionally collect information from children under 13 years of age. As we do not collect any personal information, the application can be used by people of all ages.

8. Third-Party Services

The application does NOT use third-party services that may collect information, including:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No authentication services
  • No cloud synchronization

9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can completely delete all data at any time by:

  1. Deleting lists individually within the app, or
  2. Uninstalling the app from your device

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we recommend that you review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
